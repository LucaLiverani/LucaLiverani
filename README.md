# Hi there 👋

## Why are my repositories private?

I've decided to make all my projects private.

We live in an era where LLMs and AI crawlers scan the entire internet every few minutes, and I'm starting to value my data, my intellectual activity, and my privacy. Code is a form of thought, and I'd rather not have mine silently absorbed into someone else's training set.

That said, **I'm always happy to share my work with any human who's genuinely interested**. Reach out, and I'll gladly walk you through what I'm building.

---

## What I'm currently working on

### A fully open-source data platform
A personal data platform I'm using to collect and analyze data about my own activities across the tools I use most: Spotify, YouTube, Google Maps, Google Calendar, and more. The goal is to own my data end-to-end and actually do something interesting with it.

### An open-source language learning project
A tool to help me (and hopefully others) learn languages, with a generous sprinkle of AI woven throughout. Built around the way I actually learn, not the way apps think I should.

### Personal finance tooling
A set of tools to manage my personal finances in a less dumb way: portfolio analysis, net worth tracking, expense breakdowns. All powered by my favorite assistant on the team, `claude-code`.
